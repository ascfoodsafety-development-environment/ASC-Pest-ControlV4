Head Office · Nelson Mandela Bay

Gqeberha (Port Elizabeth)

Our head office is in Gqeberha, making it our fastest-response service area. We cover the full metro — residential suburbs, CBD commercial premises, industrial zones, the harbour precinct, the airport corridor, North End, Walmer, Summerstrand, Newton Park, Lorraine, Kabega and beyond.

The city's warm, humid coastal climate creates year-round pest pressure. German cockroaches thrive in food businesses and residential kitchens; rodents are active across the harbour and industrial zones; and summer brings increased fly and ant activity. ASC's Gqeberha programmes are designed around these local conditions — with same-day emergency response for urgent infestations and compliance documentation for regulated sites.

  • Same-day emergency callout available
  • Full residential suburb coverage
  • CBD, harbour and industrial zones
  • HACCP / BRCGS / FSSC 22000 compliance programmes
  • Residential, commercial and industrial clients

Kariega (Uitenhage)

Kariega is home to one of South Africa's largest automotive manufacturing plants and a wide range of supporting industrial operations. ASC provides compliance-driven pest management for food, manufacturing and warehousing clients alongside standard residential services across the town.

Industrial sites in Kariega carry a particular risk of rodent ingress through delivery and loading infrastructure. Our rodent management programmes include numbered bait stations, entry-point proofing and regular monitoring with trend reports — meeting the documentation requirements of HACCP and food-safety auditors operating in the area.

  • Industrial and manufacturing site programmes
  • Rodent control with numbered bait stations
  • Food-safety and HACCP-aligned documentation
  • Residential cockroach, ant and rodent control
  • Rapid response from Gqeberha base
