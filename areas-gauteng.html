Why Gauteng Businesses Choose ASC

Gauteng is South Africa's economic engine — home to the largest concentration of food manufacturers, pharmaceutical producers, logistics hubs, retailers and hospitality venues in the country. The pest management stakes are correspondingly high: a single failed audit or contamination incident in a food facility can result in product recalls, lost certification and significant reputational damage.

ASC brings the same compliance rigour we've developed across Eastern Cape food processors, pack houses and regulated facilities to Gauteng clients. Our programmes are built around your specific audit standard — HACCP, BRCGS or FSSC 22000 — and deliver the documentation that passes audits, not just the treatments that eliminate pests.

Common Questions

Gauteng Service — FAQs

Do you have Gauteng-based technicians?

We service Gauteng from our Eastern Cape operation, deploying qualified technicians for site visits by arrangement. For ongoing compliance programmes, scheduled visits are coordinated to ensure regular, consistent coverage. Contact us to discuss the frequency and timing that suits your facility.

Can you support our BRCGS or FSSC 22000 audit in Gauteng?

Yes. Our programmes are specifically structured around BRCGS Clause 4.13 and FSSC 22000 pest management requirements. We provide a complete documentation pack, attend pre-audit walkthroughs and respond to corrective-action findings within agreed timeframes — exactly as we do for our Eastern Cape compliance clients.

How quickly can you respond to an urgent infestation in Gauteng?

For urgent infestations at existing Gauteng clients, we prioritise rapid response — contact us directly on 061 138 3989 or via WhatsApp. For new Gauteng enquiries, we will advise on the fastest available response and, where necessary, coordinate a trusted local partner to provide immediate assistance.

What industries do you cover in Gauteng?

We focus on commercial and industrial sectors in Gauteng — food and beverage manufacturers, pharmaceutical facilities, warehousing and logistics, hospitality and retail. For residential clients in Gauteng, we recommend contacting a locally-based specialist, though we can advise on what to look for.

Is a once-off treatment available in Gauteng, or only contracts?

Both options are available. We offer once-off knockdown treatments as well as ongoing maintenance contracts. For regulated facilities with audit requirements, an ongoing programme with documentation is strongly recommended — but we can start with a once-off assessment and discuss the right ongoing programme from there.
