Pest Control for Food & Beverage in the Eastern Cape & Gauteng

Food and beverage operations face the strictest pest-management expectations of any sector. A single sighting can fail an audit, trigger a recall or close a line. ASC delivers a fully documented Integrated Pest Management programme designed around your process flow — from goods-in and processing to packaging, cold storage and distribution. Numbered monitoring devices are mapped to your site, activity is trended over time, and every finding is logged with a corrective action, giving you the defensible evidence that HACCP, BRCGS, FSSC 22000 and IFS auditors require.

  • IPM programme mapped to your process flow
  • Numbered, audit-ready monitoring devices
  • HACCP / BRCGS / FSSC 22000 / IFS documentation
  • Trend reporting & corrective-action logs
  • Food-safe treatment methods & EFK fly control
  • Stored-product insect & rodent management

FAQ

Food & Beverage pest control — common questions

Which food-safety standards do you support?

HACCP, BRCGS, FSSC 22000 and IFS. Our device maps, trend reports and corrective-action logs are built to satisfy each of them.

Will pest control disrupt production?

No — visits are scheduled around your operating windows and use food-safe methods suited to live production environments.

How do you handle an audit?

You receive an organised documentation pack — device maps, service reports, trend graphs and corrective actions — and we can support you during the audit itself.

Do you cover cold-chain and distribution sites?

Yes. We design programmes for processing, packaging, cold storage, warehousing and distribution across the Eastern Cape and Gauteng.
