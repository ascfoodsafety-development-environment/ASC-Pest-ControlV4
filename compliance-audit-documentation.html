Pest Control Audit Documentation in the Eastern Cape & Gauteng

Auditors don't just want pests controlled they want proof. ASC's audit documentation turns every visit into defensible evidence: numbered devices mapped to your floor plan, service reports for each visit, activity trend graphs, a corrective-action register tracked to close-out, plus certificates and safety data sheets. When an inspector arrives, you hand over one organised, up-to-date pack instead of scrambling for paperwork — for HACCP, BRCGS, FSSC 22000 or any customer audit.

  • Numbered device maps per site
  • Service report for every visit
  • Activity trend graphs over time
  • Corrective-action register with close-out evidence
  • Treatment certificates & safety data sheets
  • One organised, audit-ready documentation pack

How it works

Our approach to audit documentation

A clear, documented sequence that keeps you continuously audit-ready.

  • Map. Every device is numbered and placed on your floor plan for traceability.
  • Record. Each visit captures activity, treatments and any findings.
  • Trend. Data becomes clear trend graphs that demonstrate control.
  • Close out. Findings become corrective actions tracked to completion for auditors.

FAQ

Audit Documentation common questions

Which standards is the documentation built for?

HACCP, BRCGS, FSSC 22000 and IFS — and it suits customer and retailer audits too.

Can we access records between visits?

Yes — your documentation pack is maintained and available whenever you need it, including at short notice for an audit.

Do you track corrective actions to completion?

Yes every finding becomes a logged corrective action with evidence of close-out.

Is this included with your programmes?

Documentation is core to every ASC programme and can be paired with our Monitoring & Audit Support for full coverage.
