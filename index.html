Questions, answered

Frequently Asked Questions

Which pests do you handle?

Cockroaches (German & American), rodents (rats & mice), flies, ants, termites, bed bugs, fleas, mosquitoes, stored-product and pantry pests and spider plus hospital-grade disinfection, all under Integrated Pest Management.

Do you serve both commercial and residential clients?

Yes. We protect family homes as well as regulated commercial and industrial sites food & beverage, healthcare, hospitality, retail and warehousing across the Eastern Cape and Gauteng.

Are your treatments safe for families, pets and food areas?

Yes. Our IPM approach uses targeted, registered products applied by trained technicians, with methods aligned to food-safety and infection-prevention standards.

Which areas do you cover?

Gqeberha (Port Elizabeth), Kariega, Despatch, Colchester, Jeffrey's Bay, Humansdorp, Hankey, Patensie, Kirkwood, Addo, Paterson, Alexandria, Port Alfred, Makhanda (Grahamstown), Coega and the wider Eastern Cape, with support across Gauteng.

Do you provide compliance and audit documentation?

Yes site maps, numbered devices, trend reports, corrective-action logs, service certificates and threshold-based escalation, aligned to HACCP, BRCGS and FSSC 22000.

How quickly can you respond?

We prioritise urgent infestations and compliance-driven sites with rapid response and 24/7 support. Call 061 138 3989 or WhatsApp for the soonest available slot.

What is Integrated Pest Management (IPM)?

IPM is a science-led approach combining inspection, sanitation advice, structural proofing, monitoring devices and targeted treatment controlling pests with the least necessary intervention, fully documented.

Do you service Gauteng?

Yes we provide pest control support across Gauteng. Contact us on 061 138 3989 to discuss your requirements.
