HACCP · BRCGS · FSSC 22000

Food & Beverage Processing & Distribution

Pest activity in food environments creates direct contamination risk, failed audits and regulatory action. ASC delivers compliance-driven IPM programmes aligned to HACCP, BRCGS and FSSC 22000 with numbered devices, site maps, trend reports and corrective-action logs that auditors expect. Our food-sector technicians understand the difference between a corrective action that satisfies a Grade A auditor and one that merely records the problem. We design programmes that close findings proactively, not reactively, reducing your audit exposure and protecting your certifications.

  • HACCP/BRCGS/FSSC 22000 aligned programmes
  • Numbered and mapped monitoring devices
  • Full audit documentation pack
  • Rapid corrective-action response
  • Dedicated food-safety IPM technicians

Infection Prevention

Hospitals, Clinics & Medical Facilities

Healthcare environments demand zero tolerance. Pest activity in a hospital or clinic is not merely a nuisance it is an infection-prevention failure and a patient safety issue. ASC's IPM programmes for healthcare facilities are designed around infection-prevention principles, with treatment methods chosen to minimise chemical disruption to sensitive clinical environments. We schedule treatments to avoid patient contact, communicate with infection-control teams before every visit, and produce verified service records that satisfy healthcare governance requirements. Our hospital-grade disinfection service complements pest management to provide a complete environmental hygiene solution.

  • Infection-prevention aligned treatments
  • Hospital-grade disinfection services
  • Low-disruption treatment scheduling
  • Detailed service certificates
  • Compliance with healthcare facility standards

Hotels · Lodges · Restaurants

Hospitality & Guest Experience

A single pest sighting can result in a negative review, lost bookings and reputational damage that takes months to repair. ASC provides discreet, scheduled pest management programmes that protect your guests, your brand and your health department ratings. We work around your operational schedule treating early morning or late evening to avoid disruption and cover every area of your property from kitchen and storage through to guest rooms and public spaces. Our programmes are designed to prevent pest activity before it becomes visible, with regular monitoring and rapid response protocols if activity is detected.

  • Discreet scheduled treatment programmes
  • Kitchen, dining and room coverage
  • Pre-inspection arrival protocols
  • Rapid emergency response
  • Health inspection documentation support

Schools & Universities

Education Campuses & Residential Halls

Schools and university campuses bring together large numbers of people in shared spaces canteens, residences, kitchens and waste storage areas that attract pests if not actively managed. ASC designs safe, scheduled pest management programmes that minimise disruption to learning activities and meet health-and-safety requirements for educational environments. Product selection is carefully managed to ensure safety around children and young people, with treatments conducted during off-hours wherever possible. Residential halls receive focused programmes addressing the specific pest pressures of communal living spaces.

  • Child-safe, registered product selection
  • Canteen, kitchen and bin-area focus
  • Residential hall programmes
  • Scheduled holiday-period treatments
  • Health & safety documentation

Corporate premises require pest management that is invisible to staff and visitors — professional enough to satisfy building compliance requirements, unobtrusive enough to operate without disrupting your workforce. Malls · Stores · Public Facilities

Retail & High-Footfall Public Spaces

High footfall, frequent deliveries and shared waste areas make retail environments particularly vulnerable to pest ingress. Delivery vehicles and packaging introduce pests through loading bays, while waste accumulation and food courts attract rodents and flies from outside the building perimeter. ASC's retail programmes target the highest-risk zones — loading bays, waste storage enclosures, kitchens and stockrooms — with monitoring and proofing that intercepts pests before they penetrate into public areas. Consumer health inspection documentation is provided for all retail programmes.

  • Loading bay and delivery zone focus
  • Waste area and bin enclosure management
  • Stockroom and back-of-house coverage
  • Fly and rodent control for food courts
  • Consumer health inspection documentation
